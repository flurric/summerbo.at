An exclusive Boat party—departing from the estrel

Hot Summer Nights is an exclusive party on a three-hundred person capacity boat. From the Estrel dock to the Spree and back, through the German Capital, take in the sunset and mingle on the upper deck, retreat to the lounge and dancefloor with live DJs.

FAQ

Attendance

We like to party different in Berlin, and offer you a truly Berlin Experience. Instead of another party in a club we thought that partying on a boat in the summer is a unique chance.

Early Bird tickets cost €25. When there are no more available, you can purchase a Normal ticket for €35. There are only 100 early bird tickets available. We also offer a VIP upgrade for €15. You get a special gift and a super limited VIP badge, but you also help us to make the party even more awesome.

You need to be 18 years or older on the day of the party. We check everyone's ID so that's why we need to know your date of birth. This all have to do with local laws and responsibility.

Yes! Simply login with your account details and tick the box for sponsor in your registration form. After submitting you will have to transfer the money to the bank details on the page.

On the day of the party you can pick up the badge inside the Estrel hotel during the afternoon or in the evening in the Biergarten near the boat. Please make sure you bring your ID with you. The badge is your entrance to the party so please do not lose it. There will be no tickets sold on the day itself.

There will be a dancefloor, lounge area and even a fursuit lounge. Of course you can also just chill outside with a drink and enjoy the sunset over Berlin.

We'll also be playing some games and events including fursuit limbo, dance-off, and of course drinking games and a diving contest.

Expect all kinds of music for the perfect night of lounging and chilling. Think: "Beach Club in Berlin".

The chance of getting seasick on this boat is very minimal. This is because the speed and the size of the boat make it impossible to feel any waves from other boats on the Spree. We do advise to talk with your doctor in case you get seasick very easily. It will be smooth sailing!

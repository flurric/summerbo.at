Welcome

Party with us in the heart of Berlin
Party, on a boat...

is an awesome experience. See the sunset over Berlin while you are lounging on the deck. Partying hard in the middle of the city. Every moment you look outside you see something new. For 4 hours we make sure you can relax and start partying before Eurofurence even has begun.

Location

Location of the boat party is the Mark Brandenburg. The biggest ship of the Stern und Kreis company that has many ships for cruises in Berlin. With a very big open deck and 4 saloons inside there is enough space for everyone.

Music

For a good summer night we need good music. Our DJ's will make sure that from begin till end you will be able to dance and chill. From lounge music, house, dance and the vocal classics everyone can sing along to.

Drinks

On the boat we have a fully equipped bar with all kinds of drinks available. You wanna go for a cold beer from the tap to watch Berlin? Sure, we have it! Want something refreshing after fursuiting or dancing hard? Also no problem! You can pay directly at the bar in cash so no need for tokens or special currency.

Fursuits

Do boats and fursuits work? Of course they do! We make sure that it's very easy to bring your stuff on the boat, including a fursuitlounge with basic supplies. We do understand that you maybe don't want to suit during the whole trip. Simply change out of suit and we keep guard. Also the outerdecks are perfect for cooling down and waving at the many people on the side.

Transport

For this party you don't have to travel at all! We start in the biergarten of the Estrel hotel. Perfect for everyone who wants to party and see Berlin without traveling far. The boat is also ending the tour there so no worries to get back to the hotel. Before boarding it's already possible to receive your badge and lanyard so you can enter without waiting. For more information about that, check below in the FAQ.

Registration

The registration is made as simple as possible. Do you still have questions about the registration and your question is not here? Send us a message via e-mail or Telegram.

How much does the party cost?

The early bird tickets cost €25.- and the normal tickets cost €35.-. There are only 100 early bird tickets available. After they are sold out you can still get a normal ticket. We also offer a Sponsor upgrade. You do not only get a goodie and special badge, but you also help us to make the party even more awesome! The registration stops the weekend before the party or when we are sold out.

What is the minimum age for attending?

You need to be 18 years or older on the day of the party. We check everyone's ID so that's why we need to know your date of birth. This all have to do with local laws and responsibility.

Can I order my sponsor upgrade later?

Yes that is possible. Simply login with your account details and tick the box for sponsor in your registration form. After submitting you will have to transfer the money to the bank details on the page.

Where can I pick up my badge?

On the day of the party you can pick up the badge inside the Estrel hotel during the afternoon or in the evening in the Biergarten near the boat. Please make sure you bring your ID with you. The badge is your entrance to the party so please do not lose it. There will be no tickets sold on the day itself.

Location

What kind of things are to do during the party?

We make sure you wouldn't be bored. So instead of a boring story about Berlin we build a party. With a big enough dancefloor, lounge area and even a fursuitlounge we make sure you can choose what you want to do. Of course you can also just chill outside with a drink and enjoy the sunset over Berlin.

What kind of music will be played?

Expect all kinds of music for the perfect night of lounging and chilling. Basically we create a beachclub on the Spree in Berlin. For the real dance types we make sure to also explore more music styles during the trip.

Is there a possibility that I will get seasick?

The chance of getting seasick on this boat is very minimal. This is because the speed and the size of the boat make it impossible to feel any waves from other boats on the Spree. We do advise to talk with your doctor in case you get seasick very easily.
